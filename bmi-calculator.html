About BMI Limitations
BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. It does not account for muscle mass, bone density, fat distribution, age, ethnicity, or sex differences in body composition. Athletes may appear "overweight" while being entirely healthy. Use BMI alongside waist circumference, waist-height ratio, and clinical assessment for a fuller picture.
